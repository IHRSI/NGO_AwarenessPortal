Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a beacon of hope based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We operate as a Section 8 Registered Non-Profit, officially licensed by the Central Government. Our core mission is to bridge gaps in society by uniting minds for a collective change, focusing on marginalized communities, the environment, and youth development.

Our 6 Core Projects

Working comprehensively to tackle various challenges across society.

Project Udaan

Empowering women with skills and opportunities, with over 900+ girls empowered through digital campaigns.

Project Jeev

Rescuing and supporting strays by feeding 50+ animals daily.

Project Prakriti

Protecting the environment through clean-up drives, with over 20,000+ trees planted.

Project Seva

Helping those in need with clothes, essentials, and over 50,000+ meals distributed.

Project Bachpanshala

Providing essential care and educating underprivileged children in rural areas.

Project Vikas

Helping youth grow through skill-building, having onboarded 1 Lakh+ successful interns in the past 5 years.

Through our 6 integrated projects spanning education, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and the empowerment of women and youth, InAmigos Foundation is driving holistic development. Reaching over 50,000 lives across 28 states, our collective effort is scaling true impact at a national level.
